# 🜂 Obsidian Codex

> *“What is learned in fragments may yet become a system.”*

## ✦ Prelude

**Obsidian Codex** is not a repository of answers — it is a record of experiences.

This space gathers the scattered artifacts of a computer science journey: ideas once unfamiliar, systems once opaque, languages once unreadable. Each entry reflects a moment where confusion gave way to structure, where abstraction took shape, and where understanding left its trace.

Like obsidian — born from fire, cooled into clarity — this codex is forged from intensity: errors, experiments, and iterations.

---

## ✦ What Lives Here

Within this repository, you will find:

* Explorations of programming languages — not just *how* they work, but *how they think*
* Notes on systems — operating systems, distributed architectures, and the invisible machinery beneath software
* Failures, misconceptions, and corrections — preserved as essential parts of learning
* Small revelations — the quiet moments where something finally *clicks*

Each piece is less a tutorial and more a footprint — evidence of a path walked.

---



## ✦ Structure

The repository is intentionally organic.

Topics may overlap. Notes may feel incomplete. Some ideas may appear multiple times from different angles.


---

## ✦ On Reading

If you are a visitor:

Do not expect polished documentation.
Expect raw thinking.
Expect evolution in progress.

Read not for conclusions, but for perspective.
